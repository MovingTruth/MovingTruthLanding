Each day opens with a moment. One quote — from philosophy, nature, leadership, or wherever you choose — placed quietly into the rhythm of your morning or your afternoon.

Not a feed. Not a list. A whisper.

Save what stays with you. Your favourites travel across your Apple devices. Notifications land when you want them, and quiet down when you don't. No account. Nothing stored that you don't choose.

Whether you're looking for perspective at the start of the day or a pause in the middle of it, Inner Whisper is a simple space for words that stick.
