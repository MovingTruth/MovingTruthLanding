A daily reading, drawn from four independent threads: your sun sign and its decan, the day's own number, the Chinese zodiac, and tonight's moon — reconciled into one voice, the same for you every time you and the day meet again.

Built from traditional, public-domain systems, not any single copyrighted source — and honest about which parts are old convention and which are Inner Sign's own. Three ways to read the sky are offered side by side, not one insisted upon.
